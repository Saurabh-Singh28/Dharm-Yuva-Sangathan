About the Sanatani IT Cell

The Sanatani IT Cell stands as the active digital shield of our movement — committed to protecting Sanatan Dharma in the digital space. We identify, report, and expose anti-Hindu content with documented truth and strategic vigilance. We believe that awareness is defense, and our mission is to awaken the youth to recognize and resist misinformation, cultural distortion, and digital propaganda through verified reporting.

Our Principles

  • Evidence-Based Research: Every report is backed by platform violations, screenshots, and sourced references.
  • Civilizational Vigilance:Tracking threats to dharmic values, identity, and cultural narratives in the online world.
  • Digital Preservation: Archiving instances of Hinduphobia, propaganda, and ideological attacks for awareness.
  • Dharmic Lens: Analyzing issues from a Sanatani worldview — rooted in truth, logic, and eternal values.
  • Unfiltered Resistance: Raising voice without bending to political correctness or biased mainstream silence.

Important Note

Our aim is not to provoke, but to educate and awaken. Every topic is approached with integrity, objectivity, and scholarly depth. This initiative is not driven by hate, but by duty — to preserve dharma, inform the youth, and counter the erasure of civilizational truth. All content is for awareness and resistance through documented reality, free from prejudice and rooted in dharmic clarity.
