Our Mission

This is not about trolling or targeting — this is about digital dharmic defense through truth and transparency. Every report, post, or action taken by the Sanatani IT Cell is backed by facts, clear violations, and context often ignored by biased platforms.

Our mission is not to spread hate, but to safeguard Sanatan Dharma in the digital realm — by exposing anti-Hindu agendas, misinformation, and hate speech with evidence. We aim to inform, alert, and awaken — not offend — and to preserve the dignity of our civilization through active vigilance.

About the Sanatani IT Cell

The Sanatani IT Cell stands as the active digital shield of our movement — committed to protecting Sanatan Dharma in the digital space. We identify, report, and expose anti-Hindu content with documented truth and strategic vigilance.

We believe that awareness is defense, and our mission is to awaken the youth to recognize and resist misinformation, cultural distortion, and digital propaganda through verified reporting.

Our Principles

  • Truth-Based Reporting: Every report is backed by facts and evidence
  • Strategic Vigilance: Systematic monitoring of digital platforms
  • Community Defense: Protecting dharmic values through collective action
  • Educational Awareness: Informing and awakening the community
  • Legal Compliance: Operating within legal boundaries and platform guidelines

Important Note

We operate within legal boundaries and platform guidelines. Our approach is based on documentation, evidence, and peaceful resistance through proper reporting channels. We do not engage in harassment, trolling, or any form of illegal activity. Our goal is to protect dharmic values through legitimate means.
